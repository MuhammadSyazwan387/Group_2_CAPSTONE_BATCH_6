Our Journey

From a traditional bank to a leader in sustainable finance

The Beginning

Founded as a community bank with a vision to serve local businesses and families with integrity and personalized service.

1995

Digital Transformation

Launched our first online banking platform and began integrating technology to enhance customer experience while maintaining personal touch.

2008

Sustainability Commitment

Made our first major commitment to sustainable finance by establishing our Green Banking division and setting carbon-neutral operational goals.

2015
